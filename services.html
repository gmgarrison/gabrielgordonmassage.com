Massage Services

Table Massage

Clients enjoy this work for its specificity and abundance of techniques. Great for addressing work-induced discomfort, athletic aches, and general stress.

Table is recommended for work on the head, neck and jaw.

Lotion or Oil may be used depending on the style of the massage and client preferences.

Thai Massage

Clients enjoy this work for its broad pressure, engagement of limbs and unique movement. Great for addressing chronic stiffness and immobile joints.

While deep pressure can be achieved on the table, working on the Thai Mat can allow for greater depth of work on certain broad areas.

Performed on a floor mat fully clothed. Please wear stretchy or athletic clothes to each session.
